Storage shelves & units

I Have Too Much Storage Space, Said No One Ever.


We understand how storage space is never enough - which is why our collection of storage systems and units has something to suit all homes - urban city apartments or countryside villas. Right from smaller units like the ALGOT mesh basket suitable for kitchens to bigger units like the BROR shelves with cabinets for study rooms, we have something for every storage situation. Build your own shelf by adding sections with the OMAR system - use it in your bathroom to store your bath linens or even in your kids room to store their toys - itll be right at home wherever you choose to place it.
