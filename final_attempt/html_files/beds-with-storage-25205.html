Beds with storage

Add a bed sized storage space to your room


Beds take up most of the floor space in a room. Pillows, bed linens and blankets are space-hungry items to store. The dead space under a bed is ideal to add storage space to easily tuck large items out of sight. This also makes one less place that collects dust. The ottoman storage beds from our MALM collection have underbed storage that is large enough to tuck away even an extra ironing board. The storage area is very easy to open and close.
